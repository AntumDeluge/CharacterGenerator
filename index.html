Character Sprite Generator (WIP)

This is the plan for a character sprite generator inspired by Stendhal, Liberated Pixel Cup, & Cabbit.

Sprites use a 3-frame NESW format similar to RPG Maker 2000/2K3. Frames are 24x32 or 48x64.
